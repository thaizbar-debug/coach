Health Conditions — Non-Negotiable Constraints

🦵 Chondromalacia Patella (both knees)
Cartilage wear under kneecap. Loaded knee flexion past ~90° aggravates it. All exercise selection must respect this.
Safe
Hip thrust · RDL · glute bridge · step-up · hamstring curl · cable pull-through · Bulgarian split squat (controlled, vertical shin)
Avoid
Deep squat · machine leg extension · forward-knee lunge · jump squat · plyometrics · running
⛓️ Calf → Back → Neck Kinetic Chain
Calf contracture pulls unilateral back → trapezius → cervicogenic head/neck tension. The root is the calf. Headaches are downstream.
Required every session
Calf foam roll (warm-up) · gastroc + soleus stretch · suboccipital release + levator scapulae (cool-down) · scapular stabilization (lower trap, serratus)
Avoid
Heavy shrugs · direct upper trap loading · chin-forward rowing/pulldown form
